### Flare Connectivity Summary

The flare connectivity summary is displayed by default. It aims to give a graphical overview over all flares that have occurred in the selected timeframe. The functionality is nearly identical to the [Flare Detail View Panel](/quick_start_guide#flare-detail-view)
- the topmost panel displays all flare peak times and color codes them based solely on magnetic connectivity. No SEP data is considered in this panel.
- the four remaining panels show different channels from the selected sensor. They give an idea about the energy spectrum of the SEP event. Which channels are displayed can be configured by the user.

Hover over the 👉 symbol to get more information.
