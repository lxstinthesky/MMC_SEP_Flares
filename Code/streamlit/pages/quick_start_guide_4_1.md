During the selected period, 8 flares were detected by EPT-SUN and are deemed magnetically connected. We have previously seen the same 8 flares in the [Flare Connectivity Summary](/quick_start_guide#flare-connectivity-summary) (marked in red and magenta). 

If we were to select EPT-ASUN for the same time period, we would find a total of 6 flares which are deemed connected and would be marked in red. Note that it is not required that these 6 flares are a subset of the 8 flares. Theoretically, they could be independent.

This information is conveyed by the EPT (All Direction) column, as it lists only the unique flares. Consider this example: For 22 flares, the tool deemed flares connected to SEP events. However, only 12 of them are unique. That means some flares were detected by multiple sensors.
